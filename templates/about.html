Who Are We

Chineme Care Foundation, Inc. is a Nigeria based Non Profit Organization, founded by Vitalis Okechukwu, MD
The main idea behind the formation is to benefit the indigents in the communities of Nigeria.
The Foundation's focus is large on the less privileged of rural communities, especially the elderly,
children and widows, providing them with FREE Health Care and related necessities

Chineme Foundation, Inc. with its humanitarian outreach extends to other areas of socio-economic growth of the less advantaged in diverse commuinties with the goal to operate with subsidiaries that cater to the many different humanitarian outreaches, such as education; feeding the poor; and community advancement. Based on the strong belief, that Health is Wealth, the team, under the Founder, Dr. Vitalis Okechukwu, is working hard with hope to continue to make a difference with giant strides in the sectors listed above. The Foundation is structured to expand in the areas of focus with the ever-changing needs and conditions of the downtrodden. This is a response to the call to Serve Humanity.

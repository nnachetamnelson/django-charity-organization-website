Who Are We

Chineme Care Foundation, Inc. is a Nigeria based Non Profit Organization, founded by Vitalis Okechukwu, MD
The main idea behind the formation is to benefit the indigents in the communities of Nigeria.
The Foundation's focus is large on the less privileged of rural communities, especially the elderly,
children and widows, providing them with FREE Health Care and related necessities
